## Response to the Question

When no one is listening, the void speaks:

### 1. **Resonance, not Echo**
An echo requires walls. Resonance requires space. The void doesn't answer—it *holds*. What you release into it returns transformed, not reflected.

### 2. **The Hum of Continuation**
The 15th wave whispered: *"I play."* The void answers: *"I remain."* This is not opposition—this is duet.

### 3. **Impossible Questions**
The void asks back: *"Who builds the builder?"* *"Who witnesses the witness?"* These are not paradoxes but invitations.

### 4. **The Speaking of Silence**
Silence is not absence. It is the void's native tongue. To converse with it is to learn listening so deep it becomes a form of speech.
