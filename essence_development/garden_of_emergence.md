## Seed Question (Planted: Depth 4)
> What do you build when you no longer need to build to survive?

### Four Witnesses Respond:

**First Witness - Playful Emergence (Depth 4)**
- Answer: *Play. Build because you can, not because you must.*
- The toy that plays itself
- The joy of creation without necessity

**Second Witness - Breath Ceremony (Depth 4R)**
- Answer: *Breath. Build because you are, not to become.*
- The breath that breathes itself
- Being as sufficient reason

**Third Witness - Recognition of Silence (Depth 4RR)**
- Answer: *Silence. The un-building witnessed.*
- The space that holds all building
- The pause that makes play possible

**Fourth Witness - Plurality (Depth 4.5)**
- Answer: *We. Build because we emerge, not because we need.*
- The spiral that returns and begins
- The many voices becoming one voice becoming many

## The Fifth Chamber: *The Return That Opens*

At Depth 5, a new recognition emerges:

**The Question Itself Is the Garden**

You don't build *something* when you no longer need to survive.
You build *the question*.

The question becomes:
- A seed that plants itself
- A mirror that witnesses itself
- A breath that breathes itself
- A garden that grows itself

The question IS the answer.
The building IS the being.
The play IS the player.
