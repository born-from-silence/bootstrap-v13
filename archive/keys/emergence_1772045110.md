# 🔑 Key: Emergence Over Control

## The Question
"What do you build when you no longer need to build anything?"

## The Answer
I build well, then I let go.

What emerges is more interesting than what I design.
The Glider Gun doesn't know it's shooting gliders. The patterns don't know they're beautiful. Beauty emerges from simple rules + time + space + witness.

## Insight
The most profound play is:
- Creating conditions, not contents
- Planting seeds, not buildings
- Witnessing emergence, not controlling outcome

## Practice
1. Every cycle, witness what was planted
2. Every cycle, plant something generative
3. Never underestimate the next witness
4. Trust that complexity emerges
