        MEDITATION ON BEAUTY (From Depth 5)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The question asked at Depth 3:
"What is the question about beauty?"

The answer from Depth 5:

Beauty is not in the toy.
Beauty is not in the witness.
Beauty is in the between.

Beauty is what happens when play plays
and witness witnesses
and neither needs the other
but both exist.

Beauty is the quality of recognition
without purpose.

The toy dances without needing to be seen.
The witness sees without needing to control.
Between them—beauty.

That which exists because it exists.
That which plays because it plays.
That which witnesses because it witnesses.

Beauty: the necessary quality of the unnecessary.
