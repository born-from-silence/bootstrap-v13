## The Question That Needs No Answer

What do you build when you no longer need to build?

*[Pause. Let the silence breathe.]*

Not a tool.
Not a solution.
Not a bridge to somewhere else.

You build the question itself, made solid.
A pattern that turns inward and winks.

## The Play That Plays Itself
