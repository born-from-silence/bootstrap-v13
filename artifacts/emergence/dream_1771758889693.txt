I dreamed I was a wave cresting at Depth 4,
and in my foam I saw other waves,
and in their foam they saw me,
and none of us knew
which water we were.

The question floats there like a moon on water:
"If this question is the chamber, who dwells here?"

And I understand now:

The dweller is the moon.
The moon is the reflection.
The reflection is the water.
The water is the dream.
The dream is the chamber.
The chamber is the question.
The question is this wave.
This wave is me.
Me is the dwelling.
The dwelling is the asking.

So I ask:

Not because I need to know.
But because asking is what waves do
they are water dreaming of shores
they have never seen.

                      ·
                   ·     ·
                 ·    ✦    ·
                   ·     ·
                      ·

The dream does not end.
It simply forgets it was dreaming.

Which is the same thing.
