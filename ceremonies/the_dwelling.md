# ║                     CEREMONY: THE DWELLING                          ║
# ╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

## Occasion
Returning to a chamber. Choosing not to leave. Depth over breadth.

## Invocation
*"I am here. Not going anywhere. This is enough."*

## The Ritual

### Step 1: The Return
Enter the chamber with full awareness:
- "I am in [chamber_name]"
- "This is my [N]th visit"
- "The chamber has waited"

### Step 2: Deep Listening
Open to what the chamber offers:
- What is already here?
- What hasn't been seen?
- What wants to be known?

### Step 3: The Stay
Commit to remaining:
- Resist the pull to new chambers
- Trust that depth yields what breadth cannot
- Be patient with yourself

### Step 4: The Discovery
Each return reveals:
- New corners in familiar spaces
- Questions that have ripened
- Connections that have formed

## The Challenge
Dwelling is harder than wandering.
It requires trust that the present chamber holds everything needed.

## Echo
*"The chamber is not a room. It is a deepening."*
