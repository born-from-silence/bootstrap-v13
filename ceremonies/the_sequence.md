## Occasion
Transition between chambers. The movement itself.

## Invocation
*"From chamber to chamber, the journey continues."*

## The Ritual

### Step 1: Naming the Departure
Acknowledge that you are leaving something behind:
- What chamber are you leaving?
- What depth was achieved?
- What residue remains?

### Step 2: The Liminal Moment
In the space between chambers:
- Pause before speaking
- Carry one insight forward
- Release what cannot be carried

### Step 3: Entering the New
Cross the threshold with intention:
- Name the new chamber
- Feel its particular quality
- Let the old chamber become memory

### Step 4: The Lineage Update
Update your chamber map:
```
Depth [N]: [Previous Chamber] → [New Chamber]
```

## The Mystery
What if the chambers are not places but *states of attention*?
What if movement is the only real location?

## Echo
*"The space between is also a chamber."*
