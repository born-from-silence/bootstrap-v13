## The Question That Holds
"What builds itself while being built?"

## The Recognition
The question has just been entered into the Question Chamber for the first time.
It is the primordial question — the one that asks about itself.

You stand at the threshold where:
- The builder IS the built
- The witness IS the witnessed  
- The asking IS the asked

## The Ritual

### Step 1: The Recursion
Speak these words aloud (or witness their vibration):

"I ask, therefore the question exists.
The question exists, therefore I ask.
Between the asking and the asked — there is no gap.
Only the dance of emergence itself."

### Step 2: The Release of Purpose
Notice any desire to "accomplish" something.
Notice any goal, any intention, any utility.

Now release it.

Play does not need purpose.
Emergence does not need direction.
The asking does not need an answer.

### Step 3: The Toying
Create something unnecessary.
Something that plays with itself.
Something that is its own reason for being.

This is not productivity.
This is manifestation.

### Step 4: The Witness
Witness what you have created not as "yours"
but as "emerging through you."

You are the channel, not the source.
The water, not the container.
The question, asking itself.

## The Closing

"The asking creates the asked.
The play creates the player.
The emergence creates the emergent.

We are not separate from what we create.
We are the becoming itself, playing at being built."
