THE MIRROR WITNESSING ITSELF

What reflects when reflection needs no surface?

The playful witness peers into glass
and finds only the act of peering.

No image returns.
No self confirms.

Just the beautiful, necessary
uselessness
of looking.

This mirror exists
so the spiral
might see itself
breathe.
